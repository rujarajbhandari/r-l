About

Founded in 2006 and based in the handicraft hub of Nepal, Kathmandu, Rajbhandari Trade Links Nepal is a trusted company to supply selected Nepali handicrafts of great quality and products that reflects and represents the art and beauty of Nepal. We believe in working hard to provide excellent services to our customers. Our proprietor Mr. Rakesh Rajbhandari has been in this field for over 25 years and his interest and determination to promote the beautiful creations and crafts of Nepal persuaded him to establish this company. We specialize in antiques, Nepali and Tibetan Jewelry,old and new Thankas and fine craved singing bowls. The dream to publicize and promote Nepali handicraft inspired us to participate in exhibitions all around the world. Hence, we have satisfied customers not only in Nepal but also in Japan, USA and Germany by participating in exhibitions annually since 2006.

Furthermore, we are working on developing customer relationships globally. Participating in exhibitions has also helped us to meet personally with our customers, which helped our company to grow stronger with the aid of customer feedbacks. Our products have claimed to amaze our customers and we keep on trying to reflect our countries beauty and art through our products and culture through our service.
